Chicken-Mushroom Skewer

Description

A skewer of mushrooms and poultry. All the best the wild has to offer are pierced on just one stick.

Ingredients

  • Mushroom
  • Fowl

Steps

  1. Remove skin and bone from chicken thigh if necessary
  2. Cut each thigh into 3 even portions
  3. Combine the seasonings together into one bowl
  4. Stir thoroughly to combine
  5. Add chicken in and make sure all of chicken is covered
  6. Cover and let it marinate for at least 3 hours. Overnight is preferred
  7. Remove stems from shiitake mushrooms
  8. Create slits in the cap (refer to video)
  9. Skewer 2 pieces of chicken and 1 mushroom per skewer
  10. Preheat pan on medium high heat and add a bit of cooking oil
  11. Cook skewers for 2-3 minutes per side
  12. Transfer to a baking dish with a wired rack
  13. Brush with marinade then bake for 15 minutes at 375 Fahrenheit (190 Celsius)
  14. Take out and brush with more marinade then bake for another 5-7 minutes until fully cooked
